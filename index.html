Friday's Reads

    Friday's Reads is a simple read-it-later list with a twist: your links only appear on Fridays, between 6 AM and 5 PM Eastern time.

    Save links any time during the week by pasting a URL above. They'll wait quietly until Friday morning. You can save up to nine links — if you add a tenth, the oldest one is quietly dropped to make room.

    When you click a link it opens in a new tab and removes itself from the list — no clutter, no archive. Once Friday's window closes at 5 PM, any unread links are cleared and the slate is wiped clean for next week.

    Your links are saved to this device only and do not sync across browsers or devices.
